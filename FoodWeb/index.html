Lumpia

Lumpia is a Filipino spring roll made with thin wrappers filled with meat and vegetables. It can be served fresh or fried and is often enjoyed as a snack or appetizer.

Pasta

Spaghetti is a long, thin pasta originating from Italy, typically made from wheat flour and water. It's often served with various sauces, like marinara or meat sauce, and topped with cheese, making it a popular and versatile dish.

Salad

Salad is a dish made of mixed greens and vegetables, often topped with proteins or nuts and dressed with various sauces.

Pancit Bihon

Pancit bihon is a Filipino stir-fried dish made with thin rice noodles, vegetables, and meat, flavored with soy sauce and garlic.
